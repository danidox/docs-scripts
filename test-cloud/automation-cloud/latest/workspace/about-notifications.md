**Notifications** offer ordered and prioritized information about your actions, as a user, or about the actions that you manage, as an admin.

In-app notifications are sent across tenants, via the **[Notifications](https://docs.uipath.com/test-cloud/automation-cloud/latest/workspace/exploring-the-user-interface#notifications-panel)** panel.

Email notifications are sent across tenants, to the email address that was used when creating the organization account.
:::note
**Known limitation**: The **Studio Web** tab is currently not visible on the **Preferences** > **Notifications** > **My Notifications** page. As a result, you cannot subscribe to or unsubscribe from Studio Web notification events.
:::
