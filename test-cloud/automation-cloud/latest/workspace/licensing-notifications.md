Organization administrators can choose to receive licensing notifications by event type or by severity. Events include license expiry, consumption, and overconsumption, while severity refers to **Info** or **Warn**.

These options can be accessed by selecting the bell ![](https://documentationpicturerepo.blob.core.windows.net/screenshots/screenshots/00_Action_Center_Automation_Cloud/bell_icon_notifications_panel.png) icon located on the top navigation bar of the organization page.

For general details, refer to the **[Notifications](https://docs.uipath.com/test-cloud/automation-cloud/latest/workspace/exploring-the-user-interface#notifications-panel)** section of this guide.

:::

## License expiration

### Expiration alerts

By default, you are notified 180, 90, 30, 14, 7, and 1 day before the license expiry date.

#### Grace period

Your license might include a grace period. If it does, you are notified about it at logon, and can renew the license without experiencing any service disruptions. If it does not, your robots stop working upon expiry.
