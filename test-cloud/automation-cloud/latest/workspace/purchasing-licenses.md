To extend your automation capabilities and benefit from premium services, you can sign up for a license subscription. With this subscription, you get instant access to the required resources. Moreover, you can choose between a monthly commitment that you can cancel any time or an annual commitment for a reduced rate.

## Online license purchase

We have put together a few pre-packaged license bundles that grant just the right mix of functionality for those getting started with robotic process automation (RPA). You can view the full details for each licensing plan on our [Pricing](http://uipath.com/pricing) page.

Alternatively, you can create a custom plan with as many licenses as you need.
:::note
Not all licenses that we offer are available through online license purchase. To purchase any of the license types that are not available on the purchase page, you must [contact Sales](https://www.uipath.com/company/contact-us) . For the full list of available licensing SKUs, refer to the [UiPath® Licensing](https://licensing.uipath.com/) page - the **Unified Pricing Plan** tab contains the license SKUs that are applicable for Automation Cloud™.
:::
