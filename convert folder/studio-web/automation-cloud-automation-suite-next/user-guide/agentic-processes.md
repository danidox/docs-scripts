Agentic processes enable you to model and implement complex business processes using Business Process Model and Notation (BPMN) concepts. BPMN is a set of standardized graphical representations used to illustrate business processes and information flows. Its widespread adoption helps facilitate the understanding of business activities, process management, and process improvement.

With agentic processes, you can orchestrate long running processes with complex flow control logic by integrating automated, people, agent, and decision-based tasks while leveraging UI, API, and AI-powered capabilities.

Modelling and implementing a business process in Studio Web is the first step in agentic orchestration, a comprehensive, end-to-end approach to creating, monitoring, and optimizing complex business processes. To learn more, refer to the [Maestro user guide](https://docs.uipath.com/maestro/automation-cloud/latest/user-guide/introduction-to-maestro).
