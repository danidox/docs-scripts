In the **Workspace** page, you can select the ![docs image](https://docs-dev.uipath.com/api/binary/studio-web/2/627891/549570) **See more** button next to a solution to:

* Duplicate your solution.
* Share your solution.
* Pin your solution to the top of the list.
* Open your solution in Studio Desktop.
* Download your solution as a .uis file.
* Delete your solution.
