title: "The Launchpad panel"
visible: true
slug: "the-launchpad-panel"
---

On the right-hand side, the Launchpad panel displays:

* UiPath products available to launch. You can select which products to display through the **Widgets** options of your Assistant Web policy.
* Favorite automations. To add automations to the **Favorites** section, drag and drop the automation from the **Automations** panel.
* Other custom groups. You can group several automations in the launchpad. Drag and drop the automations under the **Favorites** section, and provide a name for the newly created group.

When you select an automation, the Launchpad is replaced by the **Automation details** panel.
