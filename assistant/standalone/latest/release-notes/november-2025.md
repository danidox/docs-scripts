## November 20, 2025

**Build number: 2026.0.180**

We are increasing our Assistant version to maintain alignment with Studio and Robot versions. This update does not include any fixes.

## November 10, 2025

**Build number: 2026.0.179**

### Improvements

You can now access additional logs from Orchestrator without navigating away from Assistant. This provides deeper visibility into the execution details, helping you identify and resolve issues faster. To access them, navigate to the Jobs tab of the automation and enable **Show Robot Logs**.
