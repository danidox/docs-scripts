The **Exporting** option allows you to export an app and import it to different tenants or share it with others.

To export an app, you click the **More options** ![](/images/apps/apps-image-More_options_icon-574728f3.png) icon on any app card and select **Export** from the drop-down list.

![docs image](/images/apps/apps-docs-image-92792-9d80a62a.webp)

A `.uiapp` file will be downloaded on your machine.
