To get you started quickly, there are some sample layout apps that can be downloaded and used. Each of the samples is downloadable and can be [imported](https://docs.uipath.com/apps/automation-cloud/latest/user-guide/import#importing-an-app) as a starting point for a new app.

An App with various App templates can be found [here](https://uipath.github.io/apps-samples/Sample_Layout.uiapp). You can use this App as a starting point for accelerating your authoring experience. You can also download a specific layout from the examples below.
