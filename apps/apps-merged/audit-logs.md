Depending on the cloud offering you use, you may have access to either the unified or classic logging experience. For details, refer to [Apps feature availability](https://docs.uipath.com/apps/automation-cloud/latest/workspace-user-guide/apps-feature-availability).

In the unified logging experience, audit logs for Apps are available in the Automation Cloud Admin section, under **Audit logs** &gt; **Organization** or **Tenant**, for each organization or tenant where Apps is enabled. For more information on audit logs, refer to [About logs](https://docs.uipath.com/automation-cloud/automation-cloud/latest/admin-guide/about-logs).
