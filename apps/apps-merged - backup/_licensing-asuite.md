To determine the most suitable user license tailored to your needs, reach out to your UiPath Sales representative. Your license choice depends on the following factors:
* Number of apps to be deployed
* Number of users for each app
* Frequency of app usage internal users and external users
* License consumption may be charged extra depending on integration.
