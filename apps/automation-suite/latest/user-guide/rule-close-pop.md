---
title: "Rule: Close Pop-Over/Bottom Sheet"
visible: true
slug: "rule-close-pop"
---

Must be combined with the [Open a page](https://docs.uipath.com/apps/automation-suite/latest/user-guide/rule-open-page#rule%3A-open-a-page) rule. Use it to close the page that was opened as a pop-over or as a bottom sheet.
