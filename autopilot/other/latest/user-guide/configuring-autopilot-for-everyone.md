Configuring Autopilot for Everyone directly impacts the capabilities and experience for end users.

To customize Autopilot for end users, access the **Admin** section in your Automation Cloud® organization. Navigate to the **AI Trust Layer** card, select the **Autopilot for Everyone** tab, then choose your tenant.

Learn more about the available settings and the way you can configure them:

* [Starting prompts](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/user-guide/stating-prompts#starting-prompts)
* [Context Grounding](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/user-guide/context-grounding#context-grounding)
* [Tools](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/user-guide/automation-properties#tools)
* [Advanced settings](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/user-guide/advanced-settings)
* [License settings](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/user-guide/license-settings#license-settings)
