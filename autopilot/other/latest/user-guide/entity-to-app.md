Autopilot™ allows you to generate an app based on an existing Data Service entity. The resulting app will be mapped to the fields in your entity and will submit data to it. You can also add new pages to an app based on an entity.

![docs image](/images/autopilot/autopilot-docs-image-435954-e77026f1.webp)

To learn how Autopilot can help you build data-driven apps, visit [Using an entity to generate an app](https://docs.uipath.com/apps/automation-cloud/latest/user-guide/entity-to-app) in the Apps guide.
