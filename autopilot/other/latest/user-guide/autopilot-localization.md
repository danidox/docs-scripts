---
title: "Localization"
visible: true
slug: "autopilot-localization"
---

Autopilot can understand and respond in any language UiPath supports, regardless of your language settings. It also replies in the same language as the user prompt. Custom starting prompts, automations, and admin configurations cannot be localized.

To change languages, either:

* do it from **Preferences** > **General** settings menu,
* write your prompt in your preferred language.

The real-time translation of automation cards, once they are displayed, might require a few moments to complete.
