The Admin App is Autopilot for Everyone tool that allows business users who have workflows in their personal workspace to configure and customize starting prompts, properties, and other settings for their own automations, so that they can leverage their automations in Autopilot.

As an organization administrator, you need to download the Admin App from UiPath Marketplace, install it in UiPath Apps, and deploy it to an Orchestrator folder before business users can use it.

As a business user, you can apply your own settings to the automations you have access to from your personal workspace.
