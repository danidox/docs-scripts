:::important
This feature is unavailable for Automation Cloud™ Public Sector customers.
:::

We strongly encourage you to provide feedback for Autopilot, as your opinion drives innovation and you help Autopilot become more accurate and aligned with your needs.


:::important
Feedback may be unavailable if your admin has disabled this functionality.
:::

To offer feedback on Autopilot:

1. Go to **Autopilot Settings**.
2. Scroll down to the **Troubleshooting** section, and select **Send Feedback**. The feedback form opens.
3. Select the type of feedback you want to offer: **Bug**, **Performance Issue**, **Feature Request**, **Other Suggestion**.
4. Provide additional information in the text field.
5. The option to **Include a screenshot and text of last Autopilot interaction** is selected by default. If you want to exclude these details, clear the check box.
6. To send your opinion to UiPath, select **Submit**.
7. To dismiss the feedback, select **Cancel**.
