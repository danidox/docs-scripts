Autopilot for Microsoft Teams and Autopilot for Slack are extensions of the core Autopilot functionality, allowing you to access Autopilot capabilities directly within your Slack or Microsoft Teams environments:

* **[Autopilot for Microsoft Teams](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/user-guide/uipath-autopilot-for-microsoft-teams)**
  + Integrates Autopilot features into the Teams environment
  + Lets you access Autopilot while collaborating with your team

* **[Autopilot for Slack](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/user-guide/uipath-autopilot-for-slack)**
  + Allows you to interact with Autopilot within Slack
  + You can ask questions, request information, and trigger automations
