The **License settings** section allows you to control the consumption of AI units after the Autopilot actions included in your license plan run out. This setting affects all users in the tenant.

To configure License settings:

1. Navigate to the Automation Cloud > **Admin** > **AI Trust Layer** > **Autopilot for Everyone** tab, then choose the tenant where Autopilot for Everyone is installed.
2. Expand the **License settings** section. The **Configure Autopilot consumption of AI Units** option is displayed:
   1. **Enable**—Default option. Lets you consume from the AI units allocated to your tenant once you exhaust your license plan entitlement.
   2. **Disable**—Once the entitlement limit included in your license plan is reached, you cannot execute any Autopilot action anymore.

Restart Autopilot for Everyone for the changes to apply.
