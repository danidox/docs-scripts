---
title: "Autopilot for Teams known limitations"
visible: true
slug: "autopilot-for-teams-known-limitations"
---

The current known limitations of the UiPath Autopilot for Teams are:

* Supports running automations in unattended mode exclusively.
* Does not automatically execute automations.
* Enabling UiPath Autopilot in group chats or teams is not supported.
* Switching between conversational agents does not persist previous conversations.
