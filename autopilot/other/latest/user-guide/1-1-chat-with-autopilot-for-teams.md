The UiPath Autopilot for Teams supports an AI-powered 1:1 chat within a personal scope.

When you [install the UiPath Autopilot for Microsoft Teams app](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/user-guide/uipath-autopilot-for-microsoft-teams-setup), the 1:1 chat with Autopilot for Teams is immediately available.
