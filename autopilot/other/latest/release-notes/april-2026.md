## April 15, 2026

### Autopilot joins Automation Suite

Autopilot generative capabilities (formerly Autopilot for developers) are now available in Automation Suite (EKS/AKS and Openshift) for generating, debugging, and editing workflows: describe the desired output and let Autopilot do the work.

Additionally, for a chat-based interaction where you can contextually create, edit, and debug workflows, you can use the Autopilot chat panel in Automation Suite products, such as Studio Web and Apps.

Read more about the [generative Autopilot](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/user-guide/autopilot-for-developers) and [Autopilot chat](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/user-guide/about-autopilot-chat).
