**Release date: 21 October 2024**

## Launching Autopilot for Everyone

Put your tasks on Autopilot, then sit back, relax, and enjoy your work day.

Autopilot for Everyone marks a significant step in accelerating automation adoption across organizations. By combining AI with over 100 ready-to-use UiPath automations, it enables you to achieve more, faster, and with greater ease. Its intuitive natural language processing offers tailored assistance and suggestions, thanks to Context grounding indexes and application-specific automations.

This release impacts multiple UiPath products:

* Assistant provides the chat experience,
* Automation Cloud allows for management and configuration,
* Automation Ops holds the specific AI trust layer policy,
* Solutions Management facilitates automation deployment, and
* Apps delivers admin functionality to business user.

Additionally, Autopilot for Everyone delivers the [Enterprise version of Clipboard AI](https://docs.uipath.com/clipboard-ai/standalone/latest/user-guide/introduction), and leverages its capabilities for pasting operations.

[Explore Autopilot's potential](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/overview/about-autopilot), [steer its powers](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/everyone-admin-guide/installing-autopilot), and see how [interacting with it](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/everyone-user-guide/interacting-with-autopilot-for-everyone) can benefit you and your organization.
