## March 19, 2026

### Bring your own LLM configuration for Autopilot

You can now bring your own LLM configuration for Autopilot through the AI Trust Layer. Go to the **LLM configurations** tab to replace models or UiPath-managed subscriptions with your own.

For details, refer to [Configuring an LLM for Autopilot](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/user-guide/configuring-an-llm-for-autopilot).
