## August 11, 2025

### Autopilot chat joins Orchestrator

Autopilot chat is now available in Orchestrator, where it takes the form of an advanced search assistant. You can ask plain-language questions and receive accurate responses drawn directly from the official UiPath documentation or the web. You can quickly access setup steps, clarify concepts, or understand specific features.

While Autopilot purpose may vary in other parts of the platform, in Orchestrator, Autopilot remains focused on delivering relevant documentation-based answers through a conversational interface. For example, you can ask Autopilot:

* "How do I debug an automation in Studio?"
* "What's the best way to allocate licenses to my users?"
* "Tell me the differences between attended and unattended automations."

Ask, discover, repeat with [Autopilot chat](https://docs.uipath.com/orchestrator/automation-cloud/latest/user-guide/autopilot-chat-in-orchestrator).

## August 6, 2025

### Introducing Autopilot chat

Autopilot chat transforms the way you interact with AI in the UiPath ecosystem. Now available for for API workflows in Studio Web, it introduces a single conversational interface that stays with you throughout your task.

Whether you're building a process, refining a prompt, or troubleshooting an automation, Autopilot chat provides contextual support exactly when and where you need it. It remembers what you're working on, understands your intent, and offers relevant, custom suggestions.

By combining context retention, real-time interaction, and a unified workspace, Autopilot chat simplifies development, reduces repetition, and helps you get to better results, faster.

[Start using Autopilot. Chat](https://docs.uipath.com/autopilot/other/latest/overview/about-autopilot-chat) and chill.
